Company Profile

Reera was formed in with a small factory premise of 200 Sq. mtr. with an aim to manufacturing and supply precision press parts, structural & sheet metal parts for a multinational lift manufacturing company and a white goods manufacturing company in India. In 1990 the organization entered into manufacturing and marketing In-plant material handling equipment and storage systems. The wide spectrum of product range includes Manually Operated, A.C. Operated, Semi Battery Operated and Fully Battery Operated equipment with most advanced technology of the industry; the storage systems range includes a wide range of internationally accepted modular design concepts. 'Reera' distinguishes itself from many other manufacturers by focusing its attention on its respective customer problem of handling and storage, and the solution from its mass produced standard item or in custom made product. To get the right solution 'Reera' representatives have at their disposal an enormous reserve of technical knowhow. Now, 'Reera' is a trusted name in industries consistently committed to offer the best solution, the best quality at the best prices and the best after sales service.
